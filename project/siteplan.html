Site Plan: VerdeVida Urban Garden

This document outlines the initial plan for the development of the "VerdeVida Urban Garden" website, including its purpose, structure, visual style, and target audience.

Site Name

Name: VerdeVida Urban Garden

Description: This name reflects a project focused on sustainable urban gardening, targeting people interested in growing their own food in small or shared spaces.

Domain availability (optional): verdevida-garden.org

Site Purpose

The website will serve as an informational and educational platform for individuals interested in urban gardening. It will include practical guides, planting calendars, sustainability tips, and a form to join workshops and community events.

Scenarios

  • What types of crops are suitable for small terraces?
  • How can I register for the in-person urban garden workshops?

Color Scheme

  • Forest Green (#2a7c4f): Will be used for headers, buttons, and main links.
  • Soft Lime Green (#85bb65): Used as an accent color and for highlighted content boxes.
  • General Background: #f9f9f9 for a clean and accessible look.

Typography

  • Headings: Georgia (classic, readable, editorial style).
  • Body Text: Segoe UI or Verdana (modern, clean, easy to read on screens).
